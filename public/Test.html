About the Computer Science Department

Anne Arundel Community College’s Department of Computer Science offers three degrees with several specialization options, as well as several certificates. The department provides a foundation in computer science and mathematics. Students learn how to use the latest and greatest software development languages to make computers do amazing things. There is also lots of hands-on lab work for students to test out their skills. The department’s graduates are competent in their discipline, particularly in the areas of problem-solving, technical competency, and software development. The department has dedicated and enthusiastic instructors who work with small classes in a laboratory setting. Students receive individual attention and participate in hands-on learning. The department also offers resources for students such as Open Lab, Computer Science Club, Cyber Defense Club, Internships, and Job Search Assistance.
